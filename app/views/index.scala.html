Important This dashboard works by reading server logs and is still in relative infancy. Please be aware of its potential limitations:

  • Only traffic to www.guardian.co.uk is monitored. (Specifically, traffic from guardiannews.com is not included.)
  • It samples 10% of site traffic. Experience suggests this gives a good overall picture, but could mislead.
  • Currently, bot traffic is not excluded. This will mislead. (We’re working on this.)
